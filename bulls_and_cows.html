Bulls and Cows A puzzling game...


Instructions

Choose the length of the number the oponent will have to think of (4 is recommended).

This number will consist of non equal digits from 1 till 9 that you will have to find out.

  • If you find a digit that exists and it's in the exact position, then you've found 1 bull.
  • If you find a digit that exists but not in the exact position, then it's a cow.

Guess all the bulls and you win!
